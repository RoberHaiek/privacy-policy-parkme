Privacy Policy — ParkMe

1. Introduction
ParkMe ("we", "us", or "our") operates the ParkMe mobile application (the "App"), a peer-to-peer parking marketplace that connects private parking spot owners with drivers seeking parking in Israel. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our App.
By registering or using ParkMe, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Who We Are
Company: ParkMe

Country of Operation: Israel/Germany

Contact: rober.haiek@gmail.com

3. Who This Policy Applies To
The App is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we discover that a minor has registered, we will delete their account and data promptly.

4. Information We Collect
We collect the following categories of personal information:
4.1 Identity & Contact Information

Full name
Email address
Phone number

4.2 Verification Documents

Photographs of parking spots

4.3 Location Data

GPS location when using the App (to show nearby spots and enable navigation)
Parking spot address entered by owners

4.4 Payment Information

We use Rapyd to process payments. When you add a payment method or bank account, your payment details are transmitted directly to Stripe. We do not store raw card numbers or bank account details on our servers.
We retain transaction records (amounts, dates, parties) for legal and accounting purposes.

4.5 Usage Data

Booking history (dates, times, spots booked or listed)
App interaction data (screens viewed, actions taken)

5. How We Use Your Information
We use your personal data to:

Create and manage your account
Verify your identity and eligibility to use the platform
Facilitate bookings between owners and drivers
Process payments and transfers through Stripe Connect
Send booking confirmations, reminders, and overstay notifications
Resolve disputes between users
Comply with Israeli law and regulatory requirements
Improve the App and develop new features
Send service-related communications (we do not send marketing emails without your consent)


6. Legal Basis for Processing
Under Israeli privacy law (the Privacy Protection Law, 5741-1981) and aligned with GDPR principles where applicable, we process your data on the following bases:

Contract performance: Processing necessary to provide our service
Legal obligation: Retaining transaction and identity records as required by law
Legitimate interest: Fraud prevention, platform safety, and improving our service
Consent: For any optional communications or features


7. How We Share Your Information
We share your data only as necessary:
7.1 Between Users

When a booking is confirmed, the driver receives the parking spot address. The owner receives the driver's first name.
No sensitive documents are shared between users.

7.2 Service Providers

Rapyd — payment processing and fund disbursement
Cloud hosting providers — secure data storage
Analytics tools — aggregated, anonymized usage data only

7.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by Israeli law, court order, or government authority.
7.4 No Sale of Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

No system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights, we will notify you and the Israeli Privacy Protection Authority (Reshut HaGannt Al HaPrivatiyut) as required by law.

10. Your Rights
Under Israeli law, you have the right to:

12. Changes to This Policy
